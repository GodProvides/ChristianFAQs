Frequently Asked Questions

What is prayer?

Prayer is talking to God. It can be done anytime and anywhere. It helps you feel closer to Him.

How do I read the Bible?

Start with a short book like John. Take one chapter at a time and reflect on it.

Why does God allow suffering?

Suffering is difficult, but it helps us grow spiritually, learn compassion, and rely on God.
